NOBLE GASES

These atoms have little tendency to participate (unlike Actinides). Gases such as radon are used in radiotherapy. Purple is the colour of Kings & Queens: Nobility.

HALOGENS

These atoms are darker as you read down the group. Fluorine is pale yellow. Chlorine in green/yellow and found in swimming pools (look: badge). Iodine crystals are shiny purple.

NON METALS

These atoms like to share & gain makeup electrons. They form acidic oxides, and diamonds (Look: Earrings & necklace)

TRANSITION METALS

These atoms are hard and have a metallic luster. Gold and silver are often used in making computer parts.

ACTINIDES

Highly electropositive, these atoms have no trouble attracting others. They spontaneously ignite, making them great for creating energy in nuclear power plants.
